#File
- fulltime.csv: data of full-time workers who have GCDWORK > 0
- parttime.csv: data of part-time workers who have GCDWORK > 0


Variables:
PERSONID: HouseholdID & PersonID concatenated
VARSTRAT: ID of Strat.
GCDWORK: Linear distance to work.
WKFTPT: work full or part time. 1 - full time, 2 - part time, 3 - multiple jobs
HH_RACE: Race of respondant
  -1 = Appropriate skip
  -7 = Refused
  -8 = Don't know
  -9 = Not ascertained
  01 = White
  02 = African American, Black
  03 = Asian Only
  04 = American Indian, Alaskan Native
  05 = Native Hawaiian, other Pacific
  06 = Multiracial
  07 = Hispanic/Mexican
WKRMHM: Has the option to work from home.
  1 = yes
  2 = no
DRIVER: driver status of sample.
  1 = driver
  2 = not a driver
DTCOST: view on price of travel.
  1 = little
  2 = moderate
  3 = big issue
DTCONJ: view on highway congestion.
  1 = little
  2 = moderate
  3 = big issue
R_SEX: Respondant gender.
  1 = male
  2 = female
R_AGE: Respondant age. (needs to be grouped, WIP)
HBHUR: Urban/Rural indicator.
  S = Suburban
  TC = Town and County
  U = Urban
WRKCOUNT: # of workers in HH
HHFAMINC: Derived total HH income.
  01 = $5,000.
  +$5,000 for each group until 10.
